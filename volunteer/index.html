Who Can Vote?

Every single voice matters in this movement, but the official voting rules for the 21st Cohort of the Seychelles National Youth Assembly are strict. To cast a ballot for Tyler Nicholas, you must meet all three of the following requirements:

  • You must be a Seychellois citizen.
  • You must be a student at École Française des Seychelles (EFS).
  • You must be in 3ème, 2nde, 1ère, or Terminale.

*Even if you cannot legally vote, you can still be a vital Campaign Ambassador by sharing our message and convincing eligible EFS students to support the campaign!
