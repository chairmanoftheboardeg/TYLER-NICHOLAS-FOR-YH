My Vision for Seychelles

"Turning Your Voice Into Policy"

Youth Empowerment

For too long, the decisions impacting our future have been made without us in the room. I am running for the 21st Cohort to ensure that young Seychellois are not just consulted, but actively involved in drafting the policies that affect our lives.

My goal is to bridge the gap between local communities and the National Assembly, creating direct channels for feedback, civic engagement, and leadership opportunities. When we empower the youth to lead today, we secure a stronger Seychelles for tomorrow.

🚀

Skill Development

The global economy is evolving, and our education and training systems must keep pace. We need to move beyond traditional curriculums and focus on building pathways for technical skills, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship.

Drawing on my experience in digital services and enterprise management, I will advocate for programs that teach practical, modern skills. Whether it is web development, business management, or creative industries, our youth must be equipped with the tools to innovate and build their own success.

💡

Exchange Program

Seychelles is deeply connected to the global community, and our youth deserve the opportunity to experience it firsthand. Cross-cultural learning broadens perspectives, inspires new ideas, and builds invaluable international networks.

I will champion the expansion of regional and international exchange programs, allowing our young leaders to collaborate with peers across the globe, share our unique Seychellois heritage, and bring world-class innovations back home to our communities.
