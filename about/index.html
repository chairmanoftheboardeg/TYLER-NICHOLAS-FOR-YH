Meet Tyler Nicholas

Entrepreneur, Community Advocate, and Your Voice for the 21st Cohort.

A Proven Record of Leadership

I believe that the youth of Seychelles are not just the leaders of tomorrow—we are the innovators and change-makers of today. My journey into public service is rooted in a deep commitment to building scalable solutions that empower our communities, drive economic growth, and foster digital literacy.

I have dedicated my early career to business development and community welfare. By turning ideas into actionable policies, I want to ensure that every young Seychellois has the tools, skills, and opportunities to succeed on the global stage.

Business & Innovation

As the driving force behind the Tyler Nicholas Group, alongside co-founder Kareem Malhas, I manage dynamic subsidiaries like Falcon LTD and Blackwood Digital Services. This hands-on entrepreneurial experience shapes my practical approach to economic empowerment.

Community Action

Leadership means serving the community. Through the Tyler Nicholas Foundation and active engagement with FoodShare Seychelles, I work directly to tackle local challenges and advocate for youth-led innovations, such as Christie Esparon's project, Pretty Dazzle.

Digital Ecosystems

Combining my passion for aviation—specifically the B777—with digital management, I have built and led large-scale virtual communities including Air Seychelles Roblox and the Emirates Group Roblox (featuring Fly Dubai). These platforms are modern classrooms for youth leadership and digital literacy.

Vision for the SNYA

In the Seychelles National Youth Assembly, I will leverage this background to champion Skill Development and International Exchange Programs, turning our collective voices into concrete policy.
